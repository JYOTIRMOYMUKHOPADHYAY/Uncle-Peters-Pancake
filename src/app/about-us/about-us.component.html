OUR STORY

We always loved pancakes but the only ones we would get were the absolute basic ones. Although they tasted great but something was missing, we craved for more varieties. During one of our Paris trips we finally had a chance to explore multiple varieties and forms of pancakes! And thatâ€™s when we sat down, chalked it out and started our journey! We did take inspiration from a lot of worldwide pancake restaurants but at the same time we did our own brainstorming and landed up with some varieties of pancakes which are available nowhere else!

It took us almost a year to standardize our batter; we were not going to serve anything short of extremely soft and fluffy pancakes. Finally in the summer of 2019 we started off from a small cloud kitchen based out of Bangalore and ever since there has been no looking back!

We have won a handful of awards and features and we are currently placed at 8 cities across the country! We dream of reaching 50+ outlets by 2023
